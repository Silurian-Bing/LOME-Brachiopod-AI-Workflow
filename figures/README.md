# Figures

This folder contains ten PNG figures selected from intermediate workflow outputs. They are included only to show the kinds of diagnostic, sampling, standardization, paleogeographic, and exploratory outputs that can be produced by the workflow.

## Included figures

The files in `demo_png/` are demonstration figures rather than final publication figures. They emphasize workflow inspection, intermediate diagnostics, spatial standardization, and selected non-final analytical views.

## Not included

The following materials are deliberately excluded:

- final main figures;
- editable vector versions of final figures;
- complete figure panels used for the associated unpublished research paper;
- figures that directly expose unpublished final interpretations.

Final publication figures and complete figure source data will be released after the associated research paper is published. This folder is therefore a workflow demonstration set, not a complete research-result archive.
