# Trigger Conditions

Call this Skill when:

- palaeolongitude or palaeolatitude fields are used;
- records need plate/region assignments;
- multiple palaeogeographic sources must be compared;
- spatial interpretation depends on coordinate quality.

Do not call this Skill when:

- only non-spatial diversity curves are needed;
- coordinates are absent and no reconstruction source is available;
- expert judgement is required to choose between competing plate models.
