# Trigger Conditions

Call this Skill when:

- imported records contain genus/species names;
- synonymy may affect diversity or palaeogeographic patterns;
- `cf.` or `aff.` records are present;
- local curation must be separated from database-provided names.

Do not call this Skill when:

- the task is only raw database download;
- no authority table or explicit rule is available;
- final expert taxonomic judgement is required.
