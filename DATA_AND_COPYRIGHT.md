# Data and Copyright Notes

This repository is a public demonstration package, not a full publication data release.

## Literature

Do not store full-text copyrighted PDFs, scanned articles, publisher-formatted papers, or complete manuscript copies in this repository. The `rag/` folder uses citation metadata, short paraphrased knowledge cards, and small demonstration chunks instead.

Private full-text corpora may be useful for authorized local research, but they should remain outside the public repository.

## Data

The `data/` folder contains small public-demo raw input tables. These files are included to show how database records can enter the workflow. They are not the final cleaned or expert-curated datasets.

The following data types are intentionally excluded:

- expert-curated synonymy tables;
- final cleaned occurrence tables;
- strict-filtered analytical datasets;
- complete taxon lists;
- full PyRate input and log files;
- unpublished final figure source data.

## Figures and Results

The figures and numerical outputs in this repository are intermediate demonstration materials. They should not be interpreted as the final results of the associated research paper.

Final figures, complete source data, and publication-ready reproducibility tables are expected to be released after the associated paper is published.

## Citation Reliability

References in `rag/bibliography/core_references.csv` are intended for the public-demo RAG index. References listed in `rag/bibliography/needs_verification.csv` should not be cited as confirmed evidence until manually checked.

## Recommended Public Use

This repository may be used to inspect:

- the RAG-Skill-Agent architecture;
- how domain-specific Skills can be documented;
- how Agent roles and human review gates can be represented;
- how intermediate workflow traces can be made auditable without exposing unpublished results.

It should not be used as a substitute for the final peer-reviewed manuscript or its eventual complete data release.
